My Dream of Traveling to Moon

Author: Md. Jon

Since I was a child, I've had a huge interest in outer space. I loved looking at the stars in the night sky and wondering what those distant worlds were like. My dream was to one day travel in a spaceship from planet to planet.

In space, you experience a feeling of weightlessness. The Earth looks like a small blue marble from there. Scientists say there could be life outside of Earth. This idea makes me even more curious. I learned in chemistry that the formula for water is H2O. Just imagine, could we find water like this on another planet?

Perhaps this dream of mine will come true one day. I am waiting for that day. To learn more, you can visit the NASA website.
