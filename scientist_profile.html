Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist. He is world-famous for developing the Theory of Relativity.

Famous Formula

His most famous formula is the mass-energy equivalence formula, known as E = mc2.
Here, E = Energy, m = mass, c = the speed of light (3 * 108) m/s

He once said: 

    "Imagination is more important than knowledge.
    For knowledge is limited,
    whereas imagination embraces the entire world, 
    stimulating progress, giving birth to evolution."
